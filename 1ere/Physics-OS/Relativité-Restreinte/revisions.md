## Transformations de Lorenz

$$x' = \gamma(x - vt)$$
$$t' = \gamma(t - \frac{vx}{c^2})$$

$$x = \gamma(x' + vt')$$
$$t = \gamma(t' + \frac{vx'}{c^2})$$
